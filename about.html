🏆 About Us

Farringdon Detached U12s isn’t just a football team — we’re a family. Rooted in Sunderland, we champion youth development, inclusivity, and community pride. Every player matters. Every sponsor counts. Every match is a chance to grow.

💬 What We Stand For

  • Team Spirit: Confidence, character, and camaraderie — on and off the pitch.
  • Inclusivity: Every child deserves to shine, regardless of background or ability.
  • Respect: We honour the game, our teammates, and our community.
  • Growth: From first kicks to future dreams, we support every step.
